
\section*{LuaLaTeX Font Options}

If you compile your document with XeLaTeX or LuaLaTeX, you can easily change the font using the \texttt{fontspec} package.

For this you need to uncomment the line in the class file \texttt{docuflow.cls}:
\begin{verbatim}
\RequirePackage{fontspec}
\end{verbatim}

Also you need to compile your document with XeLaTeX or LuaLaTeX instead of PdfLaTeX.
To do go to your \texttt{settings.json} file in VSCode and change the compiler from pdflatex to lualatex.\\

For ease of use, a macro:

\texttt{\textbackslash fontX\{FontName\}\{Text\}} is provided to switch fonts.\\

Below are some examples of different fonts. Make sure you have these fonts installed on your system to see the effect.\\

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item \fontX{Arial}{\textbf{Arial:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{inter}{\textbf{Inter:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{consolas}{\textbf{Consolas:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{comic sans ms}{\textbf{Comic Sans:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{agency fb}{\textbf{Agency FB:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{exo}{\textbf{Exo:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{exo 2}{\textbf{Exo 2:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{calibri}{\textbf{Calibri:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{tahoma}{\textbf{Tahoma:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{bookantiqua}{\textbf{Book Antiqua:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{garamond}{\textbf{Garamond:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{timesnewroman}{\textbf{Times New Roman:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \fontX{georgia}{\textbf{Georgia:}\qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.}
    \item \textbf{Default:} \qquad The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog
\end{itemize}
